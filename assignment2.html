When you have bacon in your mouth, it doesn't matter who's president.

-Louis C.K., 1967-

Louis C.K. is a comedian, actor, director, producer, writer, and editor. He is American-born to a Hungarian family. His name is an Anglicisation of his Hungarian last name, Szekely. He was born in Washington, D.C., but moved to Mexico (to be specific, Mexico City), where the rest of his family lived, when he was one year old. He lived in Mexico City until he was 7. Spanish was his first language, but he has since forgotten most. His comedic career began mostly when performing his material on the shows of felow comedians such as David Letterman, Conan O'Brien, Jay Leno, and Jimmy Kimmel.
